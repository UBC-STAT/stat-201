Idea behind the course¶

Imagine: Suppose you want to know the average grade for all students who have taken this course. You survey some STAT201 students, calculate the average grade, and present the number. Whats the problem with this?

Science: present results and communicate uncertainty / evidence.

Question: What aspects of uncertainty might you present with your results?

This course is about providing evidence behind your calculated results, especially by communicating uncertainty in your numeric estimates. univariate samples calculating mean, proportion, quantile, etc. to infer about a population. Two-group comparisons leads to more advanced courses (like STAT 301)
